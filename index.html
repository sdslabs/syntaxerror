Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

Time-limited coding event. You've got < 24 hours to produce something that works and is impressive. You bring nothing but a laptop and an idea, and hopefully leave with working code. You might want to read this.

Why Syntax Error?

It's fun! It gives you an excuse to learn something new and challenge yourself. Prove your nerd-fu to ther world. What can you do in 24 hours?

Where Do I Start?

Find a small problem to solve, that you think you can solve with what you know + 2-3 new things. Find a toolset to use - either one you know or want to explore. Keep your scope limited.

How Can I Win?

Top 3 prizes are for the most complete and impressive hacks. We also have prizes for hacks that don't make it to the top 3 but stand out! For example, best design, geekiest, funniest, hardware hacks, etc.
